They are online virtual spaces where people in the form of avatars gather in search of social interaction through games, events, lectures, shows and other types of activities.

About MetaCar

It is a space in the metaverse made for car lovers, so that they can share theirs with each other and promote exclusive events for participants of this space of ours.

Installation

The community takes place within Microsoft's AltSpace VR platform and can be accessed by downloading the AltSpace app from the Windows/Mac store on your computer or via the Oculus Quest VR through its app store.

AltSpace VR is currently not available for mobile phones, tablets, but only Windows/Mac and Oculus Quest VR computers.

Step by Step

Step 01

Go to the Windows store on your computer, download and install the AltSpace VR app, or go to https://metacar.com and go to the download link. For Mac version, go to https://metacar-mac/. Instructions in more detail here.

Step 02

Once downloaded and installed, when accessing the app, log in using a Microsoft account or create a new account.

Step 03

Now create your avatar and in the "Enter code" menu, enter the code to enter the cult. The code will be available here on our website in the CULTS section of the menu.
